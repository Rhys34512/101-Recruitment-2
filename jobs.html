00001 - Front-End Web Developer

Location: Melbourne, Australia
Category: Web Development
Type: Full Time
Salary: AU$75,000 - 95,000/yr

Who are we looking for:

We are looking for a Front-End Web Developer to join the Web Development department of our online management services company. As a Web Development team member, you will work with a portfolio of market-leading, high-traffic websites with a global target audience. Sometimes, you will be required to work independently on issues, and other times with team members, but you will always have all the tools and mentoring to succeed. Work is available on continually evolving long-established projects and in any new developments.

Responsibilities

As a member of our web development you will be responsible for liasing with the customer to ensure quality across our stable of projects. You will be responsible for the forward facing elements of these projects, both past and future.

  • Partnering with the wider digital team to own and build responsive website content fit for purpose and objectives.
  • Test and evolve design functionality and optimising the experience for customers.
  • Deliver experiences that are compliant to the WCAG AA 2.1 accessibility standards.

What is expected of applicants:

  • You will be an Australian Citizen, Permanent Resident or possess the correct working Visa.
  • Practical with a modern front-end JavaScript framework.

Skills and Expertise

  • Visual Studio .NET environment using GIT within Azure DevOps
  • Strong debugging skills, with the ability to resolve, suggest and implement fixes
  • Solid hands-on knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and JSON
  • Be comfortable tweaking code for A/B tests

Advantageous skills
